Harbor Nights

Harbor Nights is an incredible event that has all the makings of a romantic date night or unique group outing. Spend an evening beside the picturesque harbor sipping select wines and trying unlimited gourmet food samples from the hotel's restaurants. Live music and traditional opera performance complete the elegant ambiance. It’s truly la dolce vita—the sweet life—at its finest.
